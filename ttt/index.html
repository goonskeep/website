hi. im the keep's dev. its dangerous to go alone, take these tips

  • prop flying is allowed as long as you're within bounds and can be shot down
  • prop killing is allowed as long as you're conforming to our rules (such as no rdm)
  • the gravity gun can pick up the rollermine, allowing you to provide yourself a semi-unbreakable prop
  • the gravity gun can pick up a wide variety of unusual things, like bodies and live grenades
  • i recommend you give the traitor weapons you're unfamiliar with a chance
  • the tau cannon's charged shot can be used similar to rocket jumping in tf2
  • the famas is very consistent for short to mid range with it's high fire rate and low recoil
  • the rifle's headshot damage bonus applies outside of zoom, so it can be used up close and personal just as effectively
  • the pistol is underappreciated, headshots are extremely effective
  • our super shotgun is overpowered, as it's right click (a machanic of "firing both barrels", in actuality it fires one shot that is statistically stronger) can 1shot a player from full health at close range
  • you might be put off by the fact we lack an awp, and instead have the VSS, but it's actually much better than an awp for many reasons
  • most people forget that they have access to a flashlight in this game, use it in dark areas

Welcome to the goon's keep, minion.

We're a TTT community built on experienced players and enjoyable vanilla-esque TTT gameplay.

Click here to connect to the server, or type connect ttt.goon.haus in the Garry's Mod console.

Guidelines

It's worth mentioning our staff's word on-server is final, as we aren't going to write up a legal document for folks to read. Generally, punishments are always up to staff discretion.

Gameplay Rules

  • No random death match (rdm)
  • No traitor baiting (t-baiting), attempting to bait someone into considering you a traitor.
  • No ghosting, the act of providing round-related or game-influencing information over outside communication while dead.
  • No hacking, or otherwise "cheating".
  • No teaming, which falls under the term metagaming, is when players "team up" with each other outside the boundries of the game. Often overlaps with ghosting.
  • No nsfw sprays, up to staff discretion where the line falls.

Social Conduct

  • Please don't spam the chat or be intentionally obnoxious.
  • Making threats against others, or the server (such as threatening ddos) won't be tolerated.
  • Discriminatory speech on the basis of race, gender, religion, etc, isn't acceptable.
  • Try not to act exceedingly toxic. This isn't league of legends.

User Ranks

Ranks are time-based and given by how long you've played on the server. You can how long you've played via the utime addon.

  • Player - 2 hours
  • Member - 6 hours
  • Regular - 12 hours
  • Veteran - 48 hours
  • Elder - 96 hours

Only staff members have colored names - anyone without it is not a staff member! All staff members can be viewed in the Staff tab.

If you feel that any of the staff are abusing their power, or if you've otherwise had issues, contact stev#0001, @youtune3#5788, or amber#9666 on discord.
